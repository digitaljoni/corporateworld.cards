The Story so far...

Dan Tenferno felt something amiss when he joined Herr E-tech Labs Limited as their new employee. He has been experiencing “daily work-life imbalance”. From what he gathers, the CEO’s unique aura has corrupted the long-time employees and morphed them into worst versions of themselves. Even the office equipments have a mind of their own! Will Dan survive this godforsaken place? Will he be corrupted, or will he escape?
